        Coming Soon

        Known gaps and planned follow-up grouped by workspace page.

        General

        • Champion catalog refresh checks are still manual; automatic release ingestion is not enabled yet.
        • Stronger admin safeguards are still needed to prevent misuse of champion tags and draft checks.

        Profile

        • Set your champion familiarity level in your personal pool.
        • Get champion pool suggestions based on your real League match history.
        • Live Riot champion stats in Profile remain a placeholder until the full implementation is shipped.

        Composer

        • Required Checks settings (composition tags) do not yet have a dedicated management surface outside Composer.
        • Dedicated Composition Checks management page with separate scoped flows (self/team/global + promotion requests).
        • Some legacy helper copy remains in Composer/Explorer and will be trimmed as controls are further consolidated.

        Teams

        • Team join request flows are API-ready, but request/review controls are not exposed in Teams yet.
        • Teams member actions require exact Riot ID entry (`GameName#Tagline`); search/autocomplete is not implemented yet.
        • Request a position on an existing team.
        • Team leads can accept or decline incoming team requests.

        Champions

        • MVP supports global champion editing tabs in Explorer (Composition, Roles, Damage Type, Scaling); self/team scoped workflows are deferred.
        • Dedicated Champion Tags management page with full catalog controls remains deferred beyond MVP.
        • Ship champion auto-ingest now that global champion-tag persistence is in place.
